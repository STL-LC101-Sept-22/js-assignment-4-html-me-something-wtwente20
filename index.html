The name of the the series I have been working on is called Project Oliver.

A brief synopsis: The story deals with the delicate topic of the afterlife.

Oliver is someone who died, but has begun reincarnating.

Oliver's reincarnations are being monitored through a group called The Order.

The Order processes departed souls, and decides whether to grant them immortality

or to remove their existence from the universe altogether.

These departed souls awaiting judgment are referred to as 'Projects'.

Belle is the member from The Order assigned with monitoring Oliver.

But we can't have a normal day at the office, now can we?

Fun Tidbit for LC101: All of Oliver's reincarnations are happening inside of a giant supercomputer.

Lots of conflict occurs and I've already spoiled so much.
